Why People Switch to FinKit

1. Privacy-First: No Data Collection

Unlike YNAB and most commercial finance apps, FinKit runs entirely in your browser. Your salary, debt amounts, and financial goals never leave your device. We have no servers, no databases, no analytics — nothing to collect your data with.

2. Truly Free — Forever

YNAB costs $14.99/month ($180/year). FinKit is MIT-licensed open source. You can use it free forever, or self-host it on Vercel in 30 seconds. The only thing we ask is buying us a coffee if it helps you.

3. No Account, No Sign-Up

Open the URL and start using it. No email, no password, no "start your free trial." Just tools that work.

4. Offline-First

Once loaded, FinKit works without an internet connection. Compare that to cloud-only tools that lock your data behind a login screen.

What FinKit Doesn't Do (And Won't Do)

We're upfront about this: FinKit doesn't connect to your bank accounts. If you want automatic transaction import, YNAB or Monarch Money are better choices. FinKit is for people who want calculators, not account aggregators — tools to plan strategy, not track every transaction.
