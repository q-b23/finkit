How Finance Apps Monetize Your Data

When a tool is "free" but not open source, here's what's likely happening:

  • Transaction data sold to marketers — your spending patterns become ad targeting profiles
  • Loan/credit card "recommendations" — these are paid affiliate placements, not objective advice
  • Aggregated data licensing — hedge funds buy spending data to predict company earnings
  • Cross-app tracking — your finance app's trackers follow you to other sites
