What Is Coast FIRE & How to Calculate It

Coast FIRE is the point where your existing investments will grow to your full FIRE number by retirement age — even if you never invest another dollar. You just "coast" by earning enough to cover living expenses. It's the most liberating milestone on the path to financial independence.

The Coast FIRE Formula

Coast FIRE is reached when:
Current Investments × (1 + Return Rate)^Years ≥ FIRE Number

Example: When Do You Reach Coast FIRE?

Assume:

  • Current age: 30
  • Target retirement age: 65 → 35 years to grow
  • Current investments: $100,000
  • FIRE number: $1,000,000
  • Expected annual return: 7%

At 7%, $100,000 compounds to ~$1,067,000 in 35 years. That exceeds the $1M FIRE target — you've already hit Coast FIRE at age 30. You don't need to save another dollar for retirement. Every dollar you earn now just needs to cover your living expenses.

How Coast FIRE Changes Your Life

  • Career flexibility: Take a lower-paying but more fulfilling job
  • Reduced stress: You're already set for retirement; work becomes optional
  • Entrepreneurship: Start a business without worrying about retirement savings
  • Part-time work: Reduce hours while investments keep growing silently
